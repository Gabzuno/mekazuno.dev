Shoplifting
Alarm System

Shoplifting is a major problem for retailers, causing big financial losses. Advanced surveillance cameras can help can help detect and prevent shoplifting in your store.

Advanced CCTV Monitoring

Advanced CCTV monitoring uses high-resolution cameras to detect shoplifters by analyzing real-time video. This method identifies suspicious activity, tracks movements, and sends alerts, enhancing security and minimizing theft.

Identifying Shoplifters

CCTV cameras use smart motion detection, facial recognition, and AI analytics to monitor and evaluate shoplifting activity in real time. These systems detect suspicious actions and track persons, improving shop security and allowing prevention and quick action.

Notification and Alarm

Upon detecting shoplifting activity, the system will automatically notify the staffs and the entire establishment by both visual alert on the monitor and an audio alarm. By this, effective and immediate response can be done which can lessen shoplifting cases.
